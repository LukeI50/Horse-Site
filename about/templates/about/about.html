Mission

"Empowering Horse Owners with Smarter Parasite Management Tools"

Our mission is to provide horse, pony, and donkey owners with accessible, evidence-based tools to assess and manage their equine worm risk.

Vision

"A Future of Healthy Horses, Effective Wormers, and Sustainable Parasite Management"

We envision a world where equine worm control is guided by science, no guesswork.

Values

  • Evidence-Based Care: We are committed to providing tools and advice rooted in the latest veterinary research, including the CANTER Guidelines.
  • Responsibility: We believe in protecting horses, their owners, and the environment by promoting sustainable worm control practices.
  • Empowerment: Our goal is to simplify complex veterinary concepts and empower horse owners to make informed decisions about their horsesâ€™ health.
  • Innovation: We leverage technology and scientific advancements to make responsible worming accessible, user-friendly, and impactful.
  • Trust and Transparency: We strive to build trust with horse owners by offering clear, honest, and unbiased information.
