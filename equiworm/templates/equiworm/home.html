Why Responsible Worm Control Matters

Blanket treatments aren't the answer! Regular, unnecessary deworming can lead to anthelmintic resistance; making treatments less effective over time. Using a risk-based approach guided by the CANTER Guidelines ensures your horse gets the right treatment when it’s truly needed. Protect your horse’s health and reduce worm burdens on your pasture.
